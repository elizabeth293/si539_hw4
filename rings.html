These are some of my favorite engagement rings.

As you can see, I love rose gold! This is the most flattering metal on me, so I'm pretty set on that detail.

I found out about Moissanite recently, and I'm convinced that this diamond alternative is for me! The stone is very close to the hardness and brilliance of a diamond, but at a fraction of the price. Most people can't tell the difference!

As for the cut of the stone, I'm less sure. I love princess cut stones, but I am also drawn to oval, radiant, cushion, and classic round cuts.

I used to think I wanted a halo ring, but after trying some on, I realized they didn't fit my hand as well as I'd imagined. I love the simplicity of the rings on this page, and I am leaning towards either a solitaire setting or a few side stones.
