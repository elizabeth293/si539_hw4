These are some of my favorite wedding venues.

I'm originally from New Mexico, and I'm very attached to the state. I found some beautiful venues there that incorporate a lot of the elements I love about the desert.

Other locations I'm interested in include: Ann Arbor (my current home), Washington (I love all the trees!), and Austin (where I grew up).

The venues I like the most are outdoor or old historical buildings. Both of these settings are already so beautiful, I'm hoping that the decor could be kept to a minimum to save costs!
