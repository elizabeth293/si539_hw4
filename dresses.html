These are some of my favorite wedding dresses.

I love a-lines dresses! The flowy skirts create an ethereal, romantic vibe that really appeals to me. I do also like a few fit-and-flares, but only if the flare is subtle and the lines are smooth.

I am a big fan of delicate lace details! I can't see myself wearing anything with much sparkle.

As for the sleeves and neckline, I'm very indecisive. These details will likely have to wait until I actually try on the dresses to see what looks best!
